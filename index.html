Song Turtle allows you to upload audio files, loop any part of the song you want, and change the playback speed. Upload an audio file to get started.

Click and drag on the waveform to select a loop. Click away from the loop to remove it. Single click anywhere on the waveform to seek.

Keys

Backspace: Delete loop
L: Start looping wherever the song is playing. L again to stop the loop
Up/Down: Change playback speed
Right/Left: Seek +/- 5 seconds
Spacebar: Play/pause
